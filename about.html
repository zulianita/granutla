Hello! I'm Patricia, creator of Granutla

I’m a super health-conscious Venezuelan gal living in the U.S. Food is my number one love — after my 2 boys of course! Ever since I was child, I’ve been tinkering with food. One day, in one of those fortunate food experiments, I came up with a blend, sampled it to some of my friends and family and Voila!, Granutla was born.

Granutla is an all-natural homemade granola. There are no chemical additives, nor preservatives, only fresh grain, fruits and nuts prepared the best way for you. Nature at its best!

Thanks for stopping by. And if you just want to say hello, that’s great too!
